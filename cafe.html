Zoo Café

The Zoo Café is the perfect spot to relax and recharge during your visit. Offering a wide variety of meals, snacks, and drinks, the café is designed to cater to families, friends, and kids alike. You can enjoy freshly prepared sandwiches, pizzas, and salads while sipping on a warm cup of coffee or a refreshing cold drink. The café has both indoor and outdoor seating with beautiful views of the zoo’s gardens, making it a comfortable place to take a break and plan the rest of your adventure. It’s also conveniently located near the entrance so you can easily stop by before or after your trip around the zoo.
