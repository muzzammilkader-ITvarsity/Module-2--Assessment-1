Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals, known for their intelligence, memory, and strong family bonds. With their trunks, they can lift heavy objects, communicate, and even show affection. At Pine City Zoo, our elephants live in a large enclosure where they enjoy mud baths, swimming pools, and plenty of space to wander. Watching them interact is both heartwarming and inspiring, as they display incredible problem-solving skills and emotions.
