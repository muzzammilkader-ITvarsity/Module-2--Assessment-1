Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals on Earth, with their long necks allowing them to reach leaves high up in trees. They are gentle giants who spend most of their day browsing on acacia trees. At Pine City Zoo, our giraffes live in a spacious enclosure designed to mimic the African savannah. Their calm and graceful movements make them a favorite among visitors, especially when they lean down to eat from the feeding platforms.
